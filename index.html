Frequently asked questions

What is a Web 3 dating app?

A Web 3 dating app is built on decentralized web technologies that utilize blockchain and cryptocurrencies to provide users with enhanced security, privacy, and control over their data.

How does the cryptocurrency aspect work in the
dating app?

The cryptocurrency aspect of the dating app allows users to make transactions, such as sending or receiving tokens, as a form of value exchange within the platform. It can be used for various purposes like unlocking premium features, tipping other users, or incentivizing certain actions like verifying profiles

How can I acquire cryptocurrency to use within the
dating app?

Cryptocurrency can typically be acquired through cryptocurrency exchanges or peer-to-peer trading platforms. Users can purchase cryptocurrencies using traditional currencies (fiat) or exchange them for other cryptocurrencies they already own.
